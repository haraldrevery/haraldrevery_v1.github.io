About Harald Revery - Biography & FAQ

Music from the forests and the mountains

Harald Revery is a contemporary electronic music producer based in Sweden. In an era of digital slop and cheap sample packs, Harald draws inspiration from the natural world, incorporating field-recorded elements ranging from nocturnal forest ambiences to the raw beauty of the world's landscapes. The haunting sounds of the forest at night to the winds at the highest mountain peaks of Scandinavia, Harald is there to capture those moments. His dedication for making music that can ignite that special spark within people is well known for the ones around him. He brings the raw spirit of Europe's nature into his productions for the world to experience.

With a decade of experience crafting tracks for the dancefloor, Harald knows how to get the rave experience anywhere â€” whether they are in a dimly lit basement bar or at a major festival. His music always hits home for those who hear it.
