Harald Revery is a contemporary electronic music producer and digital visionary based in Sweden. With a decade of experience crafting tracks for the dancefloor, Harald knows how to connect with an audience — whether they are in a dimly lit basement bar or at a major festival. His music always hits home for those who hear it.

In an era of digital slop and cheap sample packs, Harald draws inspiration from the natural world, incorporating field-recorded elements ranging from nocturnal forest ambiences to the raw beauty of the world's landscapes. The haunting sounds of the forest at night to the winds at the highest mountain peaks of Scandinavia, Harald is there to capture those moments.
